Research Projects

Deep Learning Framework for Doppler Angle Estimation from Doppler Ultrasound Images

Developed a low-complexity deep learning framework utilizing advanced Explainable AI (XAI) techniques aimed at enhancing the accuracy of Doppler angle estimation, which is critical for reliable blood flow velocity measurements and effective cardiovascular disease diagnosis.

Achieved a remarkable Dice score of 0.96 through thorough evaluation of various image segmentation models, including VGG19 + UNET, ResNet50 + UNET, and MobileNetV3 + UNET.

Implemented a robust polynomial regression model for accurate Doppler angle estimation, successfully achieving a mean error of -3.2 degrees, optimized for mobile device deployment.

High-Resource Translation: Turning Abundance into Accessibility

Presented a novel approach to constructing an English-to-Telugu translation model using transfer learning techniques, specifically by fine-tuning a pre-trained English-to-Italian translator.

Incorporated iterative backtranslation with advanced techniques such as Flash attention for memory efficiency and Byte Pair Encoding with SentencePiece (vocabulary size: 16,000) to generate synthetic parallel data.
